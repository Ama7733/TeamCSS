Lodging

Bear Ridge offers two guests a cozy, fully furnished cabin for a romantic getaway in upper Buffalo River country. The cabin has a main floor and a sleeping loft with a queen-size bed. We are pleased to offer complimentary WIFI with this cabin rental. The cabin's kitchen features a dining bar and is fully furnished with appliances, basic meal prep utensils and cookware, a coffeemaker, and even a dishwasher. In the cabin's bathroom, you'll find a lovely stone walk-in shower, along with towels, soap, and shampoo.
