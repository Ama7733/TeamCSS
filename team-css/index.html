Something Catchy

Founded in the Spring of 2022 by Bruce Bauer and Thomas Wallace, Buffalo River Adventurers was created to provide a jumping off point to explore the Buffalo National River. Established in 1972 as the nation's first National River, the Buffalo River is an outdoor enthusiasts paradise. From viewing the native Elk herd to beautiful floats on the river, there is something for everyone.

Something Catchy

Buffalo River Adventurers hope you choose to spend a relaxing weekend or longer in our beautiful cabins. We can help you with your float plans, recommend hiking and climbing excursions, and even shuttle your vehicle for you. Take a trip you'll never forget and plan your trip to the river today.
